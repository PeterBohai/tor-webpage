Tower of Rak

EARLY DEMO NOW AVALIABLE

A turned-based roguelike game inspired by Tales of Maj'Eyal and the webtoon Tower of God.

In a world of two opposing races, a young Gator hero sets out to conquer the ancient Tower of Rak, which is said to hold a legendary relic with immense powers.
The hero seeks to free the Gators from the supression of the overwhelmingly dominant Teurtal race.
